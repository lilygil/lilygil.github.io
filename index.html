Bees Newly Added to Endangered Species List

Seven types of yellow face bees have been officially listed as endangered in the United States. Researchers believe this may be due to certain incecticides that are harmful to pollinators. The label "endangered" gives the bees some newfound protection that will hopefully lead to a repair in population.


How Can You Help?

Easy ways to help include planting a garden and limiting your use of pesticides. If you don't have a green thumb you can help support local bee keepers.
